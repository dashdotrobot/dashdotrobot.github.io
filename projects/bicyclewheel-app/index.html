Bicycle wheel simulator app

bicyclewheel.info is an online, interactive app for designing and testing virtual bicycle wheels. Choose or customize your rim, set the hub dimensions, and choose your spoke pattern and properties, and then see how your wheel stands up to forces like weight, braking, pedaling, and side loads.
